Commissions

Interested in commissioning a theme song for your character or campaign? Great! I would love to work on a theme to enhance your tabletop experience. I have worked with over two hundred different players and Dungeon Masters to tailor specific songs for their table. Providing a unique instrumental for their vision and the world they are rolling their dice in. Keep scrolling down for my price sheet and a link to fill out your Dungeons and Dragons commission request.

Not for Dungeons or Dragons? That's perfect too. I accept commissions for any kind of media. In fact I love working on Film and Video Games. Whatever your vision, your game, or your project I am available for commissions both exclusive and non-exclusive. Some people have asked "what is the difference?" and I would love to explain.

Non-exclusive simply means that I keep the rights to the song once it has been completed and sent over. I can sell it elsewhere and use it however I see fit. The song is still always tailored made for my client, but this allows the commission to be more cost effective.

Exclusive means that you retain all of the rights to the song once it is completed and sent over. I will send over a document giving the rights to the song to you to use however you see fit. No one else is able to use the song for monetary gain unless you give them the right.

Whichever option you choose, you will be taken to jotform to fill out a short information sheet that will be emailed. I will respond by email within 1-2 days of receiving your commission request where we can speak more about your ideas for the theme!
