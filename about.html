A Little About Lucas

Lucas Quinn is a composer from Seattle, Washington who makes music for Dungeons & Dragons, Video Games, Film and TV. He has worked on over two hundred different commissions for D&D character and campaign themes which are tailor made for the players of the tabletop role playing game. Lucas has worked on various web comics including the first season of Realta and the main theme for Incident Report.

Additionally, he has worked on various different indie film scores providing life to film in the way of a moving, engaging score. He has worked on films such as Frostbite, Hatchet Lady Crimes, and Don't Blink. Lucas has also worked on various indie video games working as the Lead Composer for Interlight Studios working on mobile games such as Blobby and audio asset development for their internal audio library.

Lucas also works in live sound, having operated the mixing board for the Tampa based dining theater group Murder, She Wrote. He has worked as an audio intern on Chu and Blossom, audio utility for Grey Room, and boom operator for multiple different commercial projects for DigiWorks Media Group.

In any work he does, Lucas aims to provide a great score or be an asset in telling an epic story. Providing the gravitas for an emotion scene, ghastly vibes for a horror score, or blood pumping tension for an action piece. Whatever your needs to heighten your world, Lucas aims to provide.
